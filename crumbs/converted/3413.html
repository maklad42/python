3413

Cause

Prevent paper abrasion has been set.

What to Do

Prevent paper abrasion may reduce print speed.

If you continue printing with prevent paper abrasion applied, press the printer 's OK button.

If you disable this setting, select Do not print with the buttons and press the printer 's OK button to cancel printing.

Then press the printer 's Setup button, select Device settings , Print settings , and then set Prevent abrasion to OFF .
