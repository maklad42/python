1369

Cause

There is insufficient ink to perform the print head cleaning.

What to Do

Press the printer 's OK button to cancel the error.

Refill the ink of each color up to the position above the single dot (A) shown in each ink tank and then retry the cleaning.

  • Refilling Ink Tanks

Important

  • Since ink flush consumes a lot of ink, perform it only when necessary.
